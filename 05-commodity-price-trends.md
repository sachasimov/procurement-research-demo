**Subject:** Market Analysis & H2 2026 Outlook: Wood Pulp and Packaging/Label Paper  

### Executive Summary
Over the past 24 months, the global pulp and packaging paper markets have been characterized by severe divergence between inflated producer list prices and deteriorating spot market realities. While major producers successfully pushed nominal list prices to record highs in early-to-mid 2026 (e.g., European BHKP reaching $1,440/tonne), actual spot prices have softened due to massive global overcapacity and muted demand. Looking toward the second half of 2026, packaging buyers should anticipate a ceiling on pulp prices driven by 15 million tonnes of unprofitable global capacity and a 23 million tonne overcapacity in the containerboard market. However, targeted price hikes in North American and European packaging grades remain a risk due to localized input cost inflation and recent tariff disruptions.

---

### 1. Pulp Price Trends (NBSK & BHKP): Mid-2024 to Mid-2026

The last 24 months have seen a widening gap between announced list prices and net spot prices, prompting Fastmarkets to note that 78% of stakeholders view European and North American list prices as "excessively inflated" [Fastmarkets amends proposal to reset European, North American pulp prices: pricing notice](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/fastmarkets-amends-proposal-to-reset-european-north-american-pulp-prices-pricing-notice/). 

**NBSK (Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft)**
*   **North America:** Spot prices have steadily declined. In January 2025, US spot prices averaged $835–$875 per tonne [US NBSK flat as SBSK posts decline on higher supply; BHK edges up $5 per tonne - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/us-nbsk-flat-as-sbsk-posts-decline-on-higher-supply-bhk-edges-up-5-per-tonne/). By January 2026, they fell to $700–$730, and by May 2026, they dropped further to $680–$710 per tonne [US NBSK prices decline amid oversupply; European downtime and rising inventories shape pulp market](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/pulp-market-prices-oversupply-us-bleached-kraft-pulp-downtime-producer-inventories-rise/). Despite this, producers pushed list prices higher; Paper Excellence announced a $1,690 list price effective May 2026 [Paper Excellence announces price increase on NBSK and SBSK pulp, effective May 1 - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/paper-excellence-nbsk-sbsk-pulp-price-increase-may-1/).
*   **Europe:** SCA increased its European NBSK price to $1,530 in February 2026, followed by an announcement to reach $1,710 for March 2026 deliveries [SCA to Increase Price for NBSK Pulp in Europe](https://www.paperage.com/2026news/02-13-2026sca-to-increase-price-for-nbsk-pulp-in-europe.html). Actual European DAP prices hovered around 1.14–1.15 EUR/kg in Q1 2026 [Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp Price Trend, Index](https://www.procurementresource.com/resource-center/northern-bleached-softwood-kraft-nbsk-pulp-price-trends).
*   **Asia:** China's NBSK spot prices declined from ~5.55 RMB/kg in January 2026 to ~5.27 RMB/kg in March 2026 [Northern Bleached Softwood Kraft Pulp Price Trend, Index](https://www.procurementresource.com/resource-center/northern-bleached-softwood-kraft-nbsk-pulp-price-trends). The PIX China NBSK Net index sat at $690.39/tonne in early February 2026 [European pulp prices climb amid ongoing BEK and NBSK negotiations](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/european-pulp-prices-climb-amid-ongoing-bek-and-nbsk-negotiations/).

**BHKP (Bleached Hardwood Kraft / Eucalyptus)**
*   **Americas & Europe:** Suzano aggressively hiked prices in early 2026, implementing a $120/tonne increase in January [Report: Suzano to Hike Pulp Prices in January](https://www.nipimpressions.com/report-suzano-to-hike-pulp-prices-in-january-cms-19792), followed by a $50 increase in March, and another $50 increase for May 2026 [Suzano to Hike Price for Hardwood Pulp in Europe and the Americas](https://www.paperage.com/2026news/04-27-2026suzano-to-hike-price-for-hardwood-pulp-in-europe-and-the-americas.html). This pushed the European nominal reference price to a record $1,440/tonne and the North American reference to $1,670/tonne in May 2026 [Suzano Raises May Pulp Prices; European Rates Hit Peak - Golden Paper Company Limited](https://www.goldenpapergroup.com/blog/suzano-raises-may-pulp-prices-european-rates-hit-peak.html).
*   **Asia:** Suzano's increases in Asia were more muted ($20/tonne in January and March), bringing projected Chinese spot prices to $740/tonne in May 2026 [Suzano announced an increase in pulp prices in May. European nominal prices have reached a record high - Golden Paper Company Limited](https://www.goldenpapergroup.com/news/suzano-announced-an-increase-in-pulp-prices-in-may.html).

*Note on Pricing Arbitrage: Fastmarkets has delayed a planned 45% downward reset of European and North American list prices to March 2027. Buyers should note that standard discounts currently range from 2–8% for NA exports to Japan, up to 13–16% for South Korea, and frequently exceed 35% domestically [Fastmarkets amends proposal to reset European, North American pulp prices: pricing notice](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/fastmarkets-amends-proposal-to-reset-european-north-american-pulp-prices-pricing-notice/) [Global Pulp Methodology and price specifications – July 2025](https://www.fastmarkets.com/uploads/2025/07/FM_Price_Methodology_Global_Pulp_July_2025.pdf).*

### 2. Packaging and Label Paper Trends

**Containerboard & Corrugated**
*   **North America:** Prices have been highly volatile. After a $20/ton decline in February 2026, producers successfully implemented a $40/ton increase in March and a $30/ton increase in April (a net $50/ton rise) [Second North America Linerboard Price Increase In Four Months - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/second-official-linerboard-increase-in-four-months-started-in-na-as-mills-face-costs-supply-cut/). Smurfit Westrock announced a subsequent $50/ton hike effective June 1, 2026 [Producers launch second wave of containerboard price increases in 2026 | Packaging Dive](https://www.packagingdive.com/news/containerboard-price-increases-june-2026-supply-demand/819166/).
*   **Europe:** European paper packaging prices peaked at €103.07 in Q2 2025 [Paper packaging prices in a volatile market: How to keep on top of changes using price and cost indices - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/paper-packaging-prices-monitor-changes-using-price-index-and-cost-index/). By early 2026, corrugated paper prices saw an 8.9% downward movement between December 2025 and March 2026, landing at roughly $200/tonne (€0.20/kg) [Corrugated Paper Prices April 2026 | Trend & Forecast](https://www.imarcgroup.com/corrugated-paper-price-trend). However, localized tightness caused Polish white-top kraftliner to rise by €40/tonne in June 2026 [Poland UKL prices rise, RCCM prices stable for second consecutive month](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/https-www-fastmarkets-com-insights-poland-containerboard-kraftliner-prices-stable/).

**Label, Cartonboard, and Specialty Papers**
*   **Europe:** Sappi Europe implemented aggressive hikes in early 2026, including an 8% to 10% increase for Parade label papers (effective April 1), a €50/ton increase for Woodfree Coated sheets (March 16), and a €65/ton increase for Packaging & Speciality products (April 15) [Sappi Europe Announces Price Increase for Label Papers | Sappi](https://www.sappi.com/en-jp/about-us/news-and-events/sappi-europe-announces-price-increase-label-papers).
*   **North America:** The US Producer Price Index for folding paperboard box manufacturing hit a record high of 243.380 in July 2025 [United States - Producer Price Index by Industry: Folding Paperboard Box Manufacturing: Folding Paperboard Boxes, Packaging, and Packaging Components - 2026 Data 2027 Forecast 1983-2025 Historical](https://tradingeconomics.com/united-states/producer-price-index-by-industry-folding-paperboard-box-manufacturing-folding-paperboard-boxes-packaging-and-packaged-components-fed-data.html). Uncoated freesheet saw 5–9% hikes from producers like Pixelle and Sylvamo in early 2026 [The Paper Market Unfolded Q4 2025](https://specialtyprintcomm.com/blog/spc-paper-market-unfolded-q42025/).

### 3. Primary Market Drivers

**1. Capacity Shifts and Supply Shocks**
*   **Additions:** Suzano's Cerrado mill ramped up starting July 2024, adding 2.55 million tonnes of BEK capacity with an ultra-low structural cash cost of ~$100/tonne [Suzano starts up Cerrado project in Brazil - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/suzano-cerrado-project-brazil-pulp-capacity-h2-24). 
*   **Delays & Closures:** A major supply shock occurred when APP delayed its 1.4 million tonne BHK line at the OKI mill from June 2026 to late 2026/early 2027, removing 500,000 tonnes of expected 2026 capacity [Shanghai Pulp Week stalemate comes to a head as global costs begin to surge](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/shanghai-pulp-week-stalemate-comes-to-a-head-as-global-costs-begin-to-surge). Additionally, global paper/board capacity exits are estimated at 2.5 to 3.5 million tonnes for 2026 [Paper Mill Closures Drive Industry Restructuring | World Paper Mill](https://worldpapermill.com/news/paper-mill-closures-industry-restructuring-2026).

**2. Geopolitics and Tariffs**
*   A 10% universal US import surcharge (effective Feb 24 to July 24, 2026) severely disrupted trade, causing US BSK exports to China to drop to a low of 22,000 tonnes in July 2026 [Fastmarkets Pulp: Capacity curtailments needed to improve global pulp market conditions in 2026](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/pulp-capacity-curtailments-global-pulp-market-2026/). Canada retaliated with duties on $2 billion of pulp and paper products [The Impact of Tariffs on the U.S. Printing Industry](https://www.printing.org/content/2025/02/10/the-impact-of-tariffs-on-the-u.s.-printing-industry). Furthermore, US tariffs on Chinese chemicals spiked bleaching input costs by 14–21% for paper manufacturers [U.S. Tariff Impact on Specialty Pulp and Paper Chemicals Market & Key Growth Drivers 2025](https://www.globalgrowthinsights.com/blog/u-s-tariff-impact-on-specialty-pulp-and-paper-chemicals-companies-735).

**3. Logistics and Energy**
*   Global container freight rates stabilized near $2,000–$3,000/FEU in early 2026 after spiking over $10,000 in 2024–2025 due to Red Sea diversions [2026 Container Rates Surge: Delays & Costs Guide](https://www.freightamigo.com/en/blog/logistics/navigating-shipping-delays-and-rising-container-freight-rates-in-2026/). However, energy costs remain a headwind; European industrial energy prices rose 18% year-over-year through Q1 2026, and North American mills faced 8–12% power cost increases [Paper Mill Closures Drive Industry Restructuring | World Paper Mill](https://worldpapermill.com/news/paper-mill-closures-industry-restructuring-2026).

### 4. Outlook for H2 2026

**Pulp Outlook: Downside Risks Dominate**
The consensus among analysts points to a structural ceiling on pulp prices for the remainder of 2026. 
*   **Price Forecasts:** BTG Pactual revised its 2026 average pulp price forecast downward to ~$570/tonne [SunSirs: China's Pulp Market Anticipates Structural Transformation by 2026](https://www.sunsirs.com/uk/detail_news-30195.html). Other estimates place 2026 averages at ~$600/ton for hardwood and ~$705/ton for softwood [Global Pulp Market 2026: Structural Shift with Tighter Supply and Uneven Demand - Golden Paper Company Limited](https://www.goldenpapergroup.com/news/pulp-market-2026-supply-demand-outlook.html).
*   **Overcapacity Ceiling:** China's massive overcapacity in paper and board is expected to act as a hard ceiling for global pulp prices [Critical pulp industry insights: 2026 outlook and supply trends](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/critical-pulp-industry-insights-2026-outlook-and-supply-trends/). Fitch Ratings notes that ~15 million tons of global pulp capacity is currently operating at a loss, warning of "brutal" capacity reductions required to balance the market in late 2026 [SunSirs: China's Pulp Market Anticipates Structural Transformation by 2026](https://www.sunsirs.com/uk/detail_news-30195.html).

**Packaging Paper Outlook: Producer Push vs. Market Reality**
*   **Containerboard:** Despite weak fundamentals, North American producers are expected to push for further price hikes. BofA Securities and Truist Securities anticipate attempts at a second $40/ton hike in late summer/early fall 2026 to offset freight and wood inflation [Spring surprise? Containerboard prices reverse course in March. | Packaging Dive](https://www.packagingdive.com/news/containerboard-price-increase-march-2026-fastmarkets-risi/815388/) [Producers launch second wave of containerboard price increases in 2026 | Packaging Dive](https://www.packagingdive.com/news/containerboard-price-increases-june-2026-supply-demand/819166/). Fastmarkets projects NA paper packaging prices will rise an average of 7% annually across 2025–2026 [Margins driving paper packaging price increases: North America paper packaging | 2025 preview - Fastmarkets](https://www.fastmarkets.com/insights/north-america-paper-packaging-outlook-2025/).
*   **Macro Headwinds:** Buyers have strong leverage to resist these hikes. The global containerboard market faces an overcapacity of ~23 million tonnes in 2026 (70% located in Asia), and the cartonboard market faces 10 million tonnes of excess capacity [Paper packaging market outlook 2026](https://www.fastmarkets.com/uploads/2026/03/paper-packaging_market_outlook_2026_v3.pdf). Coface warns that a deteriorating global economic outlook, exacerbated by tariffs, skews production and pricing risks heavily to the downside [Paper: Sector risk analysis and economic outlook | Coface](https://www.coface.com/news-economy-and-insights/business-risk-dashboard/sector-risk-files/paper).

### Gaps and Limitations
*   **Specific Q3/Q4 2026 Futures:** While NOREXECO futures were noted as available for Q3/Q4 2026, the exact forward curve prices for those specific quarters were not present in the findings (only Q1 2026 futures at $678 for NBSK and $550 for BHKP were explicitly quantified).
*   **Label Paper Absolute Prices:** Findings provided percentage increases (e.g., 8-10% for Sappi) and Euro/tonne hikes for specialty papers, but lacked absolute baseline prices (USD/tonne) for label paper grades. 
*   **Asian Packaging Forecasts:** Specific H2 2026 price forecasts for Asian containerboard and cartonboard were limited, with data primarily focusing on capacity surpluses rather than projected price points.
